Solo Singing

Rules


  • Indian vocal solo.

    • The participant shall sing of his/her choice to showcase their talent.

    • Karaoke/CD can be played.

    • If anyone is intrested in playing instruments of their choice or willing to make anoyone play on their behalf they are free to do so. Note that there will be no extra points awarded for this.

    • Total time duration is 6+2.

    • Judge's decision is final.

  • Westren Vocal solo.

    • Maximum number of accompanist is two.

    • Language of song to be selected is only in english.

    • Duration is 6+4 minutes.

    • 4 minutes for instruments or stage setting.

    • Judges decision is final.
