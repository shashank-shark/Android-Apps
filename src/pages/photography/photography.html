Rules


  • Images that have won prizes in prior competitions or have been used for commercial purposes and been published will not be eligible.
  • Submitted images should be in JPEG format.
  • Digital manipulation that distorts the reality of the images will not be allowwed.
  • Only basic enhancements such as sharpening, contrast, adjustments or simple cropping will be allowed.
  • Each image submitted must be the original work of the participants who must also be its copyright owner.
  • The images must be captured in the campus premises and in provided time.
  • Watermark's are not allowed.
