vegetablecarving

Rules


  • Maximum members in a team are 4.
  • Participants must bring the ingredients for vegetable carving i.e. vegetables in raw or boiled state.
  • Peelers, knives and other instruments are to be bought by the participants and cutting or peeling of vegetables is to be done at the venue of the competition.
  • Things needed for vegetable carving and presentation, such as plates, trays and cloth etc., are to be bought by the participants.
