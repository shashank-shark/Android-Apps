Mime

Rules


  • Number of people in a team is 6-8,
  • Time limit for each team is 6+2 minutes.
  • There is no restriction to the concept of mime; anything with a message is welcome.
  • The judgement will be based on the qualities like idea, creativity of presentation, use of makeup, music and general impression.
  • Exceeding time will lead to negative marking.
  • Event will be judged only with the minimum of three teams.
