Info for Mad-Ads

Rules


  • Minimum 3 teams are required for the judgement.

  • Duration is 10+5 minutes (three minutes is for stage setting).

  • Maximum number of members in a team is 8+2.

  • The caution should be taken to refrain from displaying obscenity, violence, prejudice, defamation etc.Negative markings will be allocated for showcase of vulgarity.

  • Judgement will be based on

    1. Presentation.

    2. Variety.

    3. Skill.

  • Judges decision will be final.
