Rules


  • There will be a time limit of 1 hour 30 minutes.
  • Participants should be present at the venue 25 minutes prior to the start of the competition.
  • Participants are requested to bring their own cultry and utensils.
  • Participants are requested to bring all necessary ingredients for cooking.
  • Pre-cooked items, ready mixes, alcohol or intoxicating items are not allowed.
  • Only two participants will be allowed inside the venue of the competition
  • No processed juice, precooked meat, vegetables, icing is allowed.
  • Cutting/peeling of the fruits or vegetables to be done on the spot.
  • All types of sauces are allowed.
  • All marinating to be done on the spot.
  • Each participants has to prepare 2 dishes.
