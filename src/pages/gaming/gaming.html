Rules


  • Clash Royale.

    • Games will be in format of normal 1 vs 1 friendly battle within the clan.

    • One card ban rule.

    • All mobile devices must have internet connection.

    • Help from others is discouraged and any kind of malpractice will lead to disqualification.

    • Best of three matches format.

    • Decision of co-ordinators is final.

  • Mini Militia.

    • 2 in a team.

    • Knock out followed for all games.

    • Unfair play shall be disqualified.

    • Judges decision will be final.

  • Counter Strike

    • 4 in a team.

    • Games settings will be set during the event.

    • Knock out followed for all the games.

    • Unfair play will result in disqualification.

    • Judges decision is final.

  • NFS

    • Single player event.

    • A participant can register only once.

    • Using cheat codes will result in disqualification.

    • Participants enroll themselves into a tournament where in if the person wins they move on to the next round and if not they are eliminated.

    • Headphones to be bought by the participant if required.
