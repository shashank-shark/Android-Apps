Slow Drag

Rules


  • This event is based on slowest to the finish line wins.
  • Boys are required to get a geared bike and the girls an un-geared bike.
  • A participant is viable for disqualification if the person loses balance and keeps a foot down or strays from the assigned lane.
  • Event will be judged only with the minimum of 3 participants.
