Info for Treasure Hunt

Rules


  • Each team will have 4 participants.

  • Each team will be alloteed a particular color and all the clues for the team will be represented by that color.

  • Participants ahould not open the clue prior to announcement of the hunt.

  • The clues are to be found in a particular order. A team cannot skip a clue. Skipping a clue leads to disqualification of the team.

  • Do not search clues in off-limit areas.

  • The treasure will consist of 8-10 clues each one leading to the next clue. Each correctly solved clue carries 10 points and final treasure carries 50 points.

  • Time duration 30-40 minutes (Based on the number of clues will be announced when the hunt starts).
